\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Rk\+ISP1 Lens Shading Correction control. 

Due to the optical characteristics of the lens, the light intensity received by the sensor is not uniform.

The Lens Shading Correction algorithm applies multipliers to all pixels to compensate for the lens shading effect. The coefficients are specified in a downscaled table in the YAML tuning file.
