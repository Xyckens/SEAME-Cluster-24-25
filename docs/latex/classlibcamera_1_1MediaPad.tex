\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1MediaPad}{Media\+Pad} represents a pad of an entity in the media graph. 

Pads are created from the information provided by the Media Controller API in the \doxylink{structmedia__v2__pad}{media\+\_\+v2\+\_\+pad} structure. They reference the \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1MediaPad_a3b23c50ec5a8dd6a60e41e6b66d78de2}{entity()} they belong to.

In addition to their graph id, media graph pads are identified by an index unique in the context of the entity the pad belongs to.

A pad can be either a \textquotesingle{}source\textquotesingle{} pad or a \textquotesingle{}sink\textquotesingle{} pad. This information is captured in the pad flags().

Pads are connected through links. Links originating from a source pad are outbound links, and links arriving at a sink pad are inbound links. Pads reference all the \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1MediaPad_a34dd306dec37e216abc98c5fba919460}{links()} that are connected to them.
