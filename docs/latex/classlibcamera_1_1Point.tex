\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Describe a point in two-\/dimensional space. 

The \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1Point}{Point} structure defines a point in two-\/dimensional space with integer precision. The coordinates of a \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1Point}{Point} may be negative as well as positive.
