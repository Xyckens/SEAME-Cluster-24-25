\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
RGB statistics for a given zone. 

Accumulate red, green and blue values for each non-\/saturated item over a zone. Items can for instance be pixels, but also the average of groups of pixels, depending on who uses the accumulator. \begin{DoxyRefDesc}{Todo}
