struct ipu3_uapi_bnr_static_config_wb_gains_config - White balance gains

@gr: white balance gain for Gr channel. @r: white balance gain for R channel. : white balance gain for B channel. @gb: white balance gain for Gb channel.

For BNR parameters WB gain factor for the three channels [Ggr, Ggb, Gb, Gr]. Their precision is U3.13 and the range is (0, 8) and the actual gain is Gx + 1, it is typically Gx = 1.

Pout = {Pin * (1 + Gx)}.
