struct ipu3_uapi_isp_tnr3_params - Temporal noise reduction v3 parameters

@knee_y1: Knee point TNR3 assumes standard deviation of Y,U and V at Y1 are TnrY1_Sigma_Y, U and V. @knee_y2: Knee point TNR3 assumes standard deviation of Y,U and V at Y2 are TnrY2_Sigma_Y, U and V. @maxfb_y: Max feedback gain for Y @maxfb_u: Max feedback gain for U @maxfb_v: Max feedback gain for V @round_adj_y: rounding Adjust for Y @round_adj_u: rounding Adjust for U @round_adj_v: rounding Adjust for V @ref_buf_select: selection of the reference frame buffer to be used.
