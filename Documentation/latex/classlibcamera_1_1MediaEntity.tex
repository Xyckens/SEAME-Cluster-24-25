\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1MediaEntity}{Media\+Entity} represents an entity in the media graph. 

Entities are created from the information provided by the Media Controller API in the \doxylink{structmedia__v2__entity}{media\+\_\+v2\+\_\+entity} structure. They reference the \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1MediaEntity_adb91c47fdbd1b581da492d6257bb648e}{pads()} they contain.

In addition to their graph id, media graph entities are identified by a name() unique in the media device context. They implement a function() and may expose a \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1MediaEntity_a7d5f090d44cd2362d3d0f5f56a651f0c}{device\+Node()}.
