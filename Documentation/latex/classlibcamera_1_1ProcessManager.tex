\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manager of processes. 

The \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1ProcessManager}{Process\+Manager} singleton keeps track of all created \doxylink{classlibcamera_1_1Process}{Process} instances, and manages the signal handling involved in terminating processes.
