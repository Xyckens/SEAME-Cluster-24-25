\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Analogue gain constants for the exponential gain model. 

The relationship between the integer gain parameter and the resulting gain multiplier is given by the following equation\+:

$gain = a \cdot 2^{m \cdot x}$

Where \textquotesingle{}x\textquotesingle{} is the gain control parameter, and \textquotesingle{}a\textquotesingle{} and \textquotesingle{}m\textquotesingle{} are image sensor-\/specific constants.

This is a subset of the MIPI CCS exponential gain model with the linear factor \textquotesingle{}a\textquotesingle{} being a constant, but with the exponent being configurable through the \textquotesingle{}m\textquotesingle{} coefficient.

When the gain is expressed in dB, \textquotesingle{}a\textquotesingle{} is equal to 1 and \textquotesingle{}m\textquotesingle{} to $log_{2}{10^{\frac{1}{20}}}$.
